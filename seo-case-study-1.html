SEO Strategy

Implementation Strategy

Six interconnected SEO pillars executed in a prioritised sequence for compounding results.

⚡

Technical SEO Overhaul

Problem: Site crawlability & speed blocking rankings
  • Fixed robots.txt to unblock 180 key product pages from crawling
  • Implemented lazy loading, WebP image conversion, CDN setup
  • Resolved 4 redirect chains and eliminated 3xx redirect loops
  • Migrated hosting to VPS reducing TTFB from 2.8s to 0.4s
  • Canonicalized 60+ duplicate product variation URLs
📈 Impact: PageSpeed 28 → 92 · LCP 8.2s → 1.8s
📄

On-Page Optimization

Problem: Low CTR and weak keyword signals
  • Rewrote 120 title tags with power words and primary keyword front-loading
  • Optimised meta descriptions for 8%+ CTR using emotional triggers
  • Added FAQ schema to 60+ blog posts capturing PAA positions
  • Implemented Product schema on all 280 product pages
  • Added breadcrumb schema improving crawl efficiency by 35%
📈 Impact: CTR 2.1% → 8.4% · 22 Featured Snippet wins
✍️

Content Optimization

Problem: Thin content, keyword gaps, low E-E-A-T
  • Expanded 45 thin product descriptions to 800+ word SEO-rich content
  • Published 24 long-form blog posts (1800–3200 words) targeting informational intent
  • Removed/noindexed 38 low-quality tag pages hurting crawl budget
  • Added author bios and expert credentials to improve E-E-A-T signals
  • Created content hubs with cluster/pillar architecture for 6 core topics
📈 Impact: Avg time on page +68% · Pages/session +42%
🕸️

Internal Linking Strategy

Problem: 48 orphan pages, poor PageRank flow
  • Built topic cluster architecture with pillar → cluster → product linking
  • Eliminated all 48 orphan pages by connecting to relevant content hubs
  • Added contextual internal links from 80+ high-traffic blog posts to products
  • Implemented breadcrumb navigation sitewide improving crawl depth
  • Used Ahrefs link opportunities report to find high-authority internal link placements
📈 Impact: Crawl depth reduced · 38% more pages indexed
🔗

Link Building Campaign

Problem: DA 14, only 23 referring domains
  • HARO (Help a Reporter Out) outreach securing 8 high-DA editorial mentions
  • Guest posting on 12 health/wellness blogs with DR 40+ authority
  • Resource page link building via broken link strategy
  • Disavowed 34 spammy backlinks detected in GSC manual actions report
  • Local citations built across 15 niche health directories
📈 Impact: DA 14 → 31 · Ref. Domains 23 → 78
📡

Indexing & Sitemap Fix

Problem: 40% of pages not indexed by Google
  • Rebuilt XML sitemap excluding noindex pages and excluding faceted URLs
  • Submitted priority product pages manually via GSC URL Inspection
  • Added hreflang tags for regional targeting (India + UAE)
  • Fixed 14 canonical errors causing Google to ignore correct page variants
  • Monitored and resolved crawl anomalies weekly via GSC Coverage report
📈 Impact: Indexed pages 280 → 440 · +57% indexation rate

SEO Insights

Key Learnings

Strategic insights extracted from this project that apply across e-commerce SEO campaigns.

INSIGHT 01
🔬
Technical Fixes First Always
No amount of content or link building helps if Google can't crawl and index your pages. Fixing the robots.txt alone unlocked 180 pages and drove an immediate 22% traffic lift in Week 3.
INSIGHT 02
🕸️
Internal Linking Is Underrated
Connecting 48 orphan pages to relevant hubs improved crawl efficiency dramatically and pushed several product pages from position 18 to position 5 without a single new backlink built.
INSIGHT 03
⚡
Core Web Vitals = Ranking Signal
Improving LCP from 8.2s to 1.8s correlated directly with a 2.4 position average ranking improvement across all monitored keywords within 45 days. Speed is an SEO lever, not just UX.
INSIGHT 04
📝
Content Clusters Outperform Standalone Posts
6 topic clusters generated 68% of all new organic traffic. Each pillar page acted as an authority hub, passing ranking power to product pages through structured contextual linking.
INSIGHT 05
🖱️
CTR Optimization = Free Traffic
Rewriting title tags and meta descriptions lifted CTR from 2.1% to 8.4% — equivalent to generating an extra 15K monthly visits at zero additional ranking cost. SERP copy matters.
INSIGHT 06
🔗
Quality Over Quantity in Link Building
8 HARO mentions on DA 60+ domains contributed more ranking lift than 20 directory submissions combined. Relevance and authority of linking domain matters far more than volume.
